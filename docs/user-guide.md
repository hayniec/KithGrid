# KithGrid User Guide

Welcome to KithGrid — your community's digital hub for connecting with neighbors, sharing resources, and staying informed.

---

## Getting Started

### Joining Your Community

1. You'll receive an **invitation code** from your community administrator (via email or directly).
2. Go to the **Join** page (`/join`) and enter your code.
3. Fill out your profile:
   - First and last name
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Home address (optional but recommended)
4. Check your email for a **verification link** and confirm your account.
5. You're in! You'll land on the community dashboard.

### Switching Communities

If you belong to multiple communities, use the **community switcher** in the sidebar to change which one you're viewing. Your profile and settings are separate per community.

---

## Dashboard

Your dashboard (`/dashboard`) is the home screen. It shows:

- **Announcements** from your community admins and board
- **Upcoming events** at a glance
- **HOA officer directory** with contact info
- **Onboarding checklist** (for new members)

---

## Forum

**Path:** `/dashboard/forum`

The forum is where your community discusses everything from safety tips to lost pets.

- **Browse posts** by category: General, Safety, Events, Lost & Found, Recommendations
- **Create a post** — pick a category and write your message
- **Like and comment** on posts from neighbors
- **Filter** by category to find relevant discussions
- **Message someone** directly from their forum profile

---

## Messages

**Path:** `/dashboard/messages`

Private one-to-one messaging with your neighbors.

- View your **conversation list** with unread indicators
- **Start a new conversation** with any community member
- Full **message history** is preserved per conversation

---

## Neighbors Directory

**Path:** `/dashboard/neighbors`

Find and connect with people in your community.

- **Search** by name, skills, or equipment
- View neighbor profiles — what skills they offer, what equipment they're willing to share
- **Contact neighbors** directly from their profile card

---

## Events

**Path:** `/dashboard/events`

Stay on top of what's happening in your community.

- Browse **upcoming events** with date, time, and location
- **RSVP** to events and set your guest count
- See how many people are going
- Filter and sort events by date

---

## Marketplace

**Path:** `/dashboard/marketplace`

Buy, sell, or give away items within your community.

- **Browse listings** — search by keyword, filter by price
- **Post an item** for sale, trade, or free
  - Add up to 5 photos
  - Set price or mark as free/negotiable
  - Set an expiration date
- **Contact sellers** directly via email

---

## Service Professionals

**Path:** `/dashboard/services`

A community-curated list of trusted service providers.

- Browse by category: Handyman, Roofer, Landscaping, Tree Service, etc.
- See **ratings** and which neighbor recommended them
- **Call providers** directly from the listing
- **Recommend** a service provider you trust

---

## Local Guide

**Path:** `/dashboard/local`

Discover restaurants, cafes, parks, shopping, and entertainment near your community.

- Browse local spots by category
- See ratings, addresses, and descriptions
- **Add a new spot** that you recommend

---

## Community Resources

**Path:** `/dashboard/resources`

Shared facilities, tools, and equipment available to community members.

- Browse available resources (clubhouse, pool, shared tools, etc.)
- **Reserve** a resource by selecting a date and time slot
- View your confirmed reservations

---

## Settings

**Path:** `/dashboard/settings`

### Profile
- Edit your **name, address, and bio**
- Add **skills** you're willing to share (e.g., plumbing, tutoring)
- List **equipment** you're willing to lend (e.g., pressure washer, ladder)
- Upload your **avatar**

### Appearance
- Switch between **Light, Dark, or System** theme
- Show or hide the emergency tab

### Emergency Button
- Enable a **floating emergency button** on every page
- Choose which corner of the screen it appears in

### Notifications
- Toggle **email alerts** on/off
- Toggle **SMS emergency alerts**
- Set preferred alert method (text, call, or both)

### Emergency Safety Network
- Add a **personal emergency access code** (e.g., gate code, lock code)
- Add **external emergency contacts** (family, doctors)
- Set a **primary emergency contact**
- Select **medically-trained neighbors** to notify in emergencies

---

## Emergency

**Path:** `/dashboard/emergency`

For urgent situations:

- **One-tap SOS** button alerts all your configured emergency contacts
- A **3-second countdown** gives you time to cancel if pressed accidentally
- Quick shortcuts to **call 911** and **Poison Control**
- View which contacts will be notified before sending

---

## Tips

- Keep your **address** up to date — it's used in emergency alerts.
- Add your **skills and equipment** so neighbors know what help you can offer.
- **RSVP to events** so organizers can plan properly.
- Check the **forum** regularly for community updates and safety alerts.
